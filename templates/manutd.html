History

Manchester United Football Club, commonly referred to as Man United or simply United, is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR F.C., the club changed its name to Manchester United in 1902.

Team

The current squad includes world-class players such as Bruno Fernandes, Marcus Rashford, and David de Gea. The team is managed by Erik ten Hag, who took over in 2022.

Achievements

Manchester United is one of the most successful clubs in English football. The club has won a record 20 league titles, 12 FA Cups, 5 League Cups, and 3 UEFA Champions League titles, among other honors.
