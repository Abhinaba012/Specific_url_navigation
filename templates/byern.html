History

Fußball-Club Bayern München e.V., commonly known as FC Bayern Munich, is a professional football club based in Munich, Bavaria, Germany. Founded in 1900, Bayern Munich is the most successful club in German football history.

Team

The current squad includes world-class players such as Manuel Neuer, Robert Lewandowski, and Thomas Müller. The team is managed by Julian Nagelsmann, who took over in 2021.

Achievements

Bayern Munich has won a record 31 national titles, including 9 consecutive Bundesliga titles since 2013, and 20 national cups, along with numerous European titles including 6 UEFA Champions League titles.
