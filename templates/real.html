History

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded on March 6, 1902, the club has a long and illustrious history and is one of the most successful football clubs in the world.

Team

The current squad includes world-class players such as Vinicius Junior, Luka Modrić, and Thibaut Courtois. The team is managed by Carlo Ancelotti, who returned to the club in 2021.

Achievements

Real Madrid is one of the most successful clubs in football history, having won 35 La Liga titles, 20 Copa del Rey titles, and a record 15 UEFA Champions League titles, among other honors.
